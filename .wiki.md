Project Summary

The project is developing a modern, responsive website for Tronmebel, a Bulgarian furniture company that specializes in custom furniture design, manufacturing, and installation for residential and commercial spaces. The revamped website enhances user experience and accessibility, showcasing a diverse range of products and services with a contemporary design. Recent updates include a light green theme, a stylish logo, improved navigation, and the addition of product galleries and an FAQ section, resulting in a more engaging user interface.
Project Module Description

The project includes the following functional modules:

    Header: Navigation bar with the company logo and links, designed for better visibility and interaction.
    ServiceCard: Displays services offered by the company with enhanced visual features and animations.
    ProductCard: Showcases featured products with images and descriptions, now with clickable links to dedicated gallery pages.
    ProjectCard: Highlights completed projects visually with improved animations.
    ContactSection: Provides essential contact information.
    AboutSection: Component for company information, clearly separated from contact details.
    FAQSection: Newly added section with an interactive accordion-style design covering common questions.
    Footer: Contains company details and social media links, consistent with the light green theme.
